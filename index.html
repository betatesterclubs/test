Level 1 - Product Tester 
New beta testers join the club as level one Product Testers. Product Testers are offered $5 per free product they test and have limited ability to select which products they want to receive and test.
Level 2 - Trusted Reviewer
Trusted Reviewers are beta testers who have proven their ability to offer useful analysis and feedback on products. They are given priority in choosing which products they want to test and offered higher ticket items to test as well as additional payouts.
Level 3 - Influencer
Influencers have proven their opinions are highly valuable to the market and people listen and follow what they say. Beta testers reaching this level are offered significantly larger payouts for product testing content include demonstrations videos, unboxing videos and testimonials.
Level 4 - Brand Partner
Brand Partnerships are reserved for the most elite beta testers within the club.
